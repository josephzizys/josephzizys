Two dancers..

31.01.2013

The solitary body on the stage, animated by the creative will, seeking beauty, seeking truth, many kinds of beauty, many kinds of truth.

I love to watch the dancers dance. My too large frame and misshapen curves are no more suited to graceful movement than my feeble mind is suited to maintaining html. Oh well.
